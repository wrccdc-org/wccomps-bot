Incident Report Guidelines

Teams are strongly encouraged to provide incident reports for each Red Team incident they detect. Incident reports can be completed as needed throughout the competition and presented to the White Team for collection.

Your incident report must contain:

  • A description of what occurred (including source and destination IP addresses, timelines of activity, passwords cracked, access obtained, damage done, etc.)
  • A discussion of what was affected
  • A remediation plan

Important: A thorough incident report that correctly identifies and addresses a successful Red Team attack may reduce the Red Team penalty for that event. No partial points will be given for incomplete or vague incident reports.
